How To Play:

Hello and thank you for playing Match Madness!
The object of the game is to match up the characters behind the boxes
These characters are revealed when the player clicks on the boxes one at a time.
If the match is correct, then the pair will stay on screen and you may continue to find new matches, but if its wrong the boxes re-appear and you need to try again.
Try to finish the game as fast as you can, and compete against your friends and family to see who is the best!
